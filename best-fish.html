Best Fish Species for Small Tanks and Beginners

This curated list includes species that thrive in smaller environments and are suitable for beginners.

  • Betta Fish - Known for their vibrant colors and flowing fins, bettas are ideal for small tanks and can be kept solo.
  • Neon Tetra - Small, colorful fish that do well in schools of six or more.
  • Guppy - Hardy and easy to care for, guppies come in a variety of colors and thrive in groups.
  • Zebra Danio - Very active and resilient, these fish can adapt to many water conditions.
  • Corydoras Catfish - Bottom dwellers that help keep the tank clean, they prefer to be in groups.
  • Cherry Shrimp - Not a fish, but they are easy to care for and add color and activity to a small tank.
  • Pygmy Sunfish - A peaceful species that stays small; perfect for nano tanks.
